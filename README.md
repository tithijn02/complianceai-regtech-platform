# ⚖️ ComplianceAI: AI-Powered RegTech for SMEs

Compliance is one of the biggest hidden barriers to growth for small businesses. This project explores how AI can transform regulatory compliance from a manual, fragmented process into a seamless, automated system.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) face increasing regulatory pressure but lack the resources to manage compliance effectively.

Key challenges:
- 60% of SMEs struggle to keep up with regulatory changes  
- GDPR penalties reached €1.2 billion in 2024  
- AML fines increased by 156% in a single year  
- Existing RegTech tools are designed for large enterprises, not SMEs.

As a result, SMEs rely on manual processes, increasing risk, inefficiency, and cost.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**ComplianceAI** is a smart, AI-powered compliance platform designed specifically for SMEs.

It provides:
- automated regulatory updates  
- plain-language compliance guidance  
- real-time risk and fraud monitoring  
- deadline alerts and audit trails  
- seamless integrations with payroll, accounting, and CRM systems  

The goal:  
👉 make compliance **simple, accessible, and scalable**

---

## 📊 Market Opportunity

The RegTech market is rapidly growing:

- $12B market (2023) → projected $84B by 2033  
- 20%+ annual growth  
- only ~18% SME adoption in the UK  

This highlights a massive **untapped SME opportunity**. 

---

## 🛠️ How it was built

The project combines market analysis, product design, and business strategy:

### 1. Market & Competitor Analysis
- Evaluated major players (ComplyAdvantage, Alloy, NICE Actimize)  
- Identified gaps in SME usability, pricing, and accessibility (page 6–8)

### 2. Product Design
Designed an SME-first platform with:
- mobile-first dashboard  
- self-service onboarding  
- AI-driven compliance intelligence  

### 3. User Journey (Production Flow)
Mapped the full lifecycle:
- discovery → sign-up → AI personalization → dashboard → integration → retention  

(visualized in the flow diagram on page 11)

### 4. Business Model
- subscription-based pricing (£29–£149/month)  
- add-ons for expert support  
- partnerships with accountants, legal firms, and APIs (page 13)

### 5. Value Proposition
- reduces compliance complexity  
- prevents costly penalties  
- saves time and operational effort  
- enables SMEs to focus on growth
